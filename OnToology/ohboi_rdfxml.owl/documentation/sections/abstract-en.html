Abstract

O(h)BOI!, an Open Human Bacterial Infectious disease Ontology, was created to provide a knowledge model of diseases caused by bacterial agents, and is proposed as means of epidemiological control of such diseases. The ontology was designed following the LOD methodology for implementing it. The present work also performed evaluations at several levels described by the NeoN methodology, since errors might have been introduced in the conceptualization and implementation phases.
